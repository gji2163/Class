Separated from Murong Yuling, Chen Ye went offline and ES real world.

Then, he turned on the computer and prepared to inquire about the copy strategy of the 12th floor of the Samsara Tower.
As for the copy of the 12th floor of the Reincarnation Tower, Chen Ye already knew that it was a large copy of “Avengers 2: Age of Ultron”.
However, Chen Ye didn’t know what the task of the dungeon was.

Although the strategies on the Internet are not good in Chen Ye’s eyes, they have reference value.

At least after reading it, Chen Ye can make a plan in advance to brush the copy.

Still very useful.

Chen Ye opened the Reincarnation Forum.

In the forum, someone was already discussing him, and tonight he made a super **** evaluation.

Netizens did not praise him, because everyone was numb to his supernatural evaluation...

The discussion in the post is all about speculation, how Shura got the super **** evaluation.

Almost most people think that Shura has broken into the lair of “Transformers: the planet of Cybertron.

Seeing this, Chen Ye smiled slightly.

In the world of “Transformers”, the distance between the planet Cybertron and the earth is measured in light years.

Chen Ye has no space jumping technology, how could he get to the planet Cybertron?

Of course, Chen Ye would not jump out to explain these people’s guesses.
